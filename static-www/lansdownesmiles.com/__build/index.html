Welcome to the home of Lansdowne Family & Cosmetic Dentistry – your place for friendly dental care in Lansdowne, VA. We happily treat patients of all ages, from children to seniors, and protect the health and beauty of their smiles.  Dr. Paul Ellington, our experienced and gentle dentist, treats every patient with the personalized attention they need for successful oral health care. 

Cosmetic and Restorative Dentistry in Lansdowne, VA

Your Lansdowne dental practice specialize in cosmetic dentistry and dental restorations for new and improved smiles. Cosmetic dental treatments help our adult patients get smiles that look healthy and vibrant, while restorations allow teeth to remain strong and promote healthy oral function.  Our most popular cosmetic and restorative therapies include:

• Porcelain veneers
• Dental crowns
• Teeth whitening
• Fixed bridges
• Composite dental fillings

Dr. Ellington also helps patients achieve and maintain excellent oral health with routine preventive care and maintenance, such as twice yearly teeth cleanings and even periodontal care to protect the health of your gums.  Our team assists patients in preventing the negative effects of gum disease and dental caries that cause damage to teeth and surrounding tissue.

Preventing Dental Problems and Promoting Improved Wellness

Our whole team is committed to helping patients achieve improved wellness. We teach healthy brushing and flossing habits to young patients to promote good dental habits from early on.  Our Lansdowne dental office also caters to senior dental patients, helping them keep their natural teeth and enjoy a solid foundation of oral health and function. We provide tips, advice, and treatments that are specifically geared to help senior patients enjoy beautiful smiles.

We have roots in our valued community. As many of you know, our doctors also practice in Potomac Falls, where every year for the last 6 years they have participated, along with their staff, in "Give Kids a Smile Day". This year they saw 13 deserving patients and donated over $10,000 of dental services. Well done, everyone!
