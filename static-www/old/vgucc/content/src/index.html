Get the Medical Care You Need Right Away!

Visit Virginia Gateway Urgent Care Center for assistance

Dealing with a minor wound or injury? Want to screen for any potential health issues? In need of a physical before starting a brand new job? Get the medical attention you need at Virginia Gateway Urgent Care Center in Gainesville. Since 2011, our knowledgeable staff and doctors have been the top choice for many individuals in need of comprehensive medical care. We offer a range of general health care services as well as medical emergency assistance. Get the care you need immediately – come to Virginia Gateway Urgent Care Center for treatment as soon as possible.

Quick assistance for walk-ins and appointments

Whether you have a scheduled screening or a medical emergency, you’ll receive exceptional care from our medical staff. We provide prompt assistance for both appointments and walk-ins, so you can rest easy knowing you’ll be taken care of right away. Schedule an appointment with our specialistsfor screenings, physicals, x-rays, etc. by calling 703-754-9111, or come by our facility anytime for urgent medical care in Gainesville.

Come to Virginia Gateway for high-quality services

At Virginia Gateway Urgent Care, we have outstanding equipment and other available resources at our facility. You will receive great care from our specialists that exceeds your expectations of quality health care services. We provide a handful of the following services for those in need:

• Urgent care
• Pre-employment physical exams
• On-site X-rays
• Drug screening & testing
• Physicals
• Immigration
• Aviation
• DOT Physicals

Don’t compromise on care – get the absolute best from the team at Virginia Gateway Urgency Care. You can use this website to get an overview of our general services. Then contact or come by right away for exceptional medical care at our urgent care in Gainesville.
