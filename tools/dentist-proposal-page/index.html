Web Design/Build Process

Phase 1 Prep Phase

  • - Complete design questionnaire during kickoff call
  • - Kickoff call with project manager
  • - Provide website content (if required)

1 Business Day

This phase can not be completed until we can complete the kickoff call and design questionnaire. If you delay the kickoff call, it will delay the process by the same amount of days.

Phase 2 Design Phase

  • We design your website based on
    your questionnaire, kickoff call,
    content, branding, and goals.

7-10 Business Days

Once the site is ready for your review, your project manager will call to go through the design in detail. This phase can take longer than 7-10 business days if you delay in providing us needed information.

Phase 3 Review and Revise Phase

  • You submit your revisions to the
    design, in list format via email to
    your project manager.

3-5 Business Days

During this phase, we complete your revision requests. This phase can take longer if you delay providing us your list of revisions or provide an extremely long list of revisons.

Phase 4 Final Review

  • You review the design & submit final
    revisions if any (up to 3 rounds of
    revisions are included free if needed).

3-4 Business Days

This phase can take longer if additional rounds of revisions are requested. Generally, 2-3 extra business days per round of revisions. If you need more than the 3 rounds of revisions included, it's fine, we just quote you hourly at that point for additional work.

Phase 5 GO LIVE!

  • - You approve site to go live
  • - Must provide domain info
     in order to take site live

Take Website Live!

Once we are given access to your domain & final approvals to take the website live it takes about 24-48 hrs. for your domain to propagate. If 301 redirects are required it could extend this process.

12-15 Business Days

This turnaround time can be longer if you’re not responsive, delay providing content, require additional rounds of revisions, delay approvals, etc. That being said, 85% of clients finish inside this time range.

*If your website takes longer than 12-15 business days it's not a problem. Take your time knowing all your un-used marketing fuel rolls forward for usage when you're ready.
